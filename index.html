About Me

Welcome to my homepage! I'm Yuhao Zhan, currently a third-year undergraduate student at the College of Computer Science and Technology, Zhejiang University (ZJU).

My research interests focus on Multimodal Reasoning, World Models, and Embodied AI. I am also exploring Agents, Self-evolving Systems, and aiming to cultivate a unique research taste in artificial intelligence.

I am fortunate to be advised by Prof. Zemin Liu and supervised by Prof. Fei Wu at ZJU. Additionally, I benefit from the guidance of Prof. Chao Huang (HKU) and Prof. Zhiyuan Liu (Tsinghua University) during my research internships.
