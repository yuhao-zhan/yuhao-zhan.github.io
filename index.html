Short Bio

I am a third-year undergraduate student at the College of Computer Science and Technology, Zhejiang University.

My research interests focus on Multimodal Learning, World Models, and Embodied AI. I believe text alone is insufficient for AGI, and I'm committed to building more general embodied intelligence.

My research taste is to pursue systematic, fundamental work with simplest methods — and build systems the community can actually use.

I am fortunate to be advised by Prof. Zemin Liu and Prof. Fei Wu at Zhejiang University. I am also grateful to be advised by Prof. Chao Huang at The University of Hong Kong and Prof. Zhiyuan Liu at Tsinghua University during my research internships.
