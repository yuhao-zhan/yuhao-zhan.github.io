Welcome to my homepage! I'm Yuhao Zhan, currently a second-year undergraduate from College of Computer Science and Technology, Zhejiang University.

My current research interests are mainly about Natural Language Processing and Automatic Evaluation. Specifically, I focus on the overcorrection issue in Grammatical Error Correction (GEC). I'm also interested in multimodal models and trying to explore different research topics in LLM.

I am fortunate to be advised by Prof. Zemin Liu and supervised by Prof. Fei Wu from College of Computer Science and Technology, Zhejiang University.
